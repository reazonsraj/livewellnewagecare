Building Your Future

Skills for a
More Independent Life

Capacity building under the NDIS refers to supports designed to help participants develop the skills, knowledge, and confidence needed to lead more independent, fulfilled, and engaged lives.

Instead of focusing solely on day-to-day assistance, capacity building empowers you to enhance your capabilities — whether that's managing finances, improving communication, fostering social connections, or participating in employment.
