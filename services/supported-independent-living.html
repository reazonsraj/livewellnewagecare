At Live Well Care And Support, we believe that everyone deserves a safe and stable place to call home. Our SIL and housing support services are designed to help NDIS participants navigate the complexities of finding and maintaining suitable accommodation.

With in-depth knowledge of the NDIS framework, we are uniquely equipped to assist participants in finding housing that supports their independence — whether they're currently homeless, at risk, or looking for their next step.

Who We Support

We Help People in
All Situations

🏠
Individuals Experiencing Homelessness
If you're without a permanent home, we provide tailored support to help you transition into stable housing. Our team collaborates with local housing providers and government programs to identify the best options.
⚠️
People at Risk of Homelessness
Housing insecurity can be overwhelming. We offer preventative support for those at risk of eviction or displacement — from advocacy to connecting you with financial assistance programs.
♿
NDIS Participants Seeking Housing
Navigating the public housing system can be complex. We specialise in helping NDIS participants access housing that meets their specific needs, ensuring accessibility and appropriate locations.
Our Housing Services

How We Help You

🔍
Housing Referrals
We connect you with local housing providers and programs to find a place that suits your unique circumstances.
📢
Housing Advocacy
We act as your voice — advocating on your behalf with housing authorities and negotiating fair outcomes.
📝
NDIS Housing Applications
We guide you through every step of the public housing process, ensuring all documentation is complete.
🚨
Emergency Housing
In crisis situations, we connect you with temporary housing and help transition to permanent arrangements.
💰
Financial Assistance
We link you with rental assistance, bond loans, and financial aid programs to ease the burden of securing housing.

Provider Network
Our connections with local providers, non-profits, and government services offer a wide range of housing solutions.
🏡
24/7 SIL Support
Round-the-clock support in shared or individual living arrangements to build independence at home.
💛
Compassionate Approach
We understand how challenging housing issues can be and treat every client with care and deep respect.
