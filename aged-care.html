Personal Care

Daily Assistance
With Dignity

Our personal care services help older adults maintain their hygiene, grooming, and daily routines with professional, respectful support. We understand that personal care requires trust, and our workers are trained to deliver it with sensitivity.

  • Bathing, showering, and personal hygiene
  • Dressing and grooming assistance
  • Oral health and skin care
  • Continence management and support
  • Morning and evening routines
  • Mobility and transfer assistance
Domestic Support

A Clean, Comfortable
Home

Maintaining a clean and safe home environment is essential for wellbeing. Our domestic support services take the pressure off daily household tasks, giving you more time and energy for what matters most.

  • General house cleaning and vacuuming
  • Laundry, ironing, and linen changes
  • Meal planning, preparation, and cooking
  • Grocery shopping and errands
  • Dishwashing and kitchen hygiene
  • Waste management and recycling
Social Support

Staying Connected
& Engaged

Loneliness and social isolation can have a serious impact on health and wellbeing. Our social support services bring meaningful companionship, conversation, and connection directly to you.

  • In-home companionship and social visits
  • Accompanying to community events and outings
  • Support to maintain hobbies and interests
  • Assistance with video calls and staying in touch
  • Engagement in day programs and groups
  • Support for social activities and appointments
Transport

Safe, Reliable
Transport Support

Getting to appointments, social activities, and everyday destinations shouldn't be a barrier. Our transport support ensures you can get where you need to go safely and comfortably.

  • Medical and allied health appointments
  • Shopping and errands
  • Social outings and activities
  • Community programs and day centres
  • Family and social visits
  • Accompanied travel and wheelchair-accessible transport
Medication Support

Health Monitoring
& Medication Help

Managing medications can be complex. Our trained care workers provide reminders, prompts, and monitoring support to keep you safe, healthy, and on schedule.

  • Medication reminders and prompts
  • Assistance with self-administration
  • Health monitoring (blood pressure, weight)
  • Coordination with GPs and pharmacists
  • Medication storage and organising dosette boxes
  • Reporting changes in health to family or clinicians
Home Maintenance

A Safe Home
Environment

A well-maintained home is a safe home. We assist with light maintenance tasks and garden upkeep so your environment stays safe, pleasant, and functional.

  • Garden care and lawn mowing
  • Watering plants and weeding
  • Minor home repairs and light maintenance
  • Window cleaning and outdoor tidying
  • Changing light bulbs and smoke alarm checks
  • Home safety assessments and hazard removal
