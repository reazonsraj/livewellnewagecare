What We Provide

Six Ways We Support
Life at Home

All services are delivered in your home by experienced, vetted care workers — consistently matched to you so you build genuine, trusted relationships.

🩺
Daily Living
Personal Care
Dignified, respectful support with personal hygiene and daily routines — delivered with care and sensitivity.
  • Bathing, showering & personal hygiene
  • Dressing and grooming assistance
  • Oral health and skin care
  • Continence management
  • Morning and evening routines
  • Mobility and transfer assistance
🧹
Home Support
Domestic Support
Keeping your home clean, safe, and comfortable so you can focus on what matters most to you.
  • House cleaning and vacuuming
  • Laundry, ironing & linen changes
  • Meal planning, prep & cooking
  • Grocery shopping and errands
  • Dishwashing and kitchen hygiene
  • Waste management and recycling
💬
Social Wellbeing
Social Support
Meaningful companionship and connection to reduce isolation and keep you engaged in life.
  • In-home companionship & visits
  • Accompanying to community events
  • Support for hobbies and interests
  • Video calls and staying connected
  • Day programs and group activities
  • Social outings and appointments
🚗
Getting Around
Transport
Safe, reliable transport so getting around is never a barrier to living your life.
  • Medical & allied health appointments
  • Shopping and errands
  • Social outings and activities
  • Community programs & day centres
  • Family and social visits
  • Wheelchair-accessible transport
💊
Health Support
Medication Support
Reminders, prompts, and health monitoring to keep you safe and on schedule every day.
  • Medication reminders and prompts
  • Assistance with self-administration
  • Health monitoring (BP, weight)
  • Coordination with GPs & pharmacists
  • Organising dosette boxes
  • Reporting health changes to family
🏡
Home Safety
Home Maintenance
Light maintenance and garden upkeep to keep your home safe, pleasant, and functional.
  • Garden care and lawn mowing
  • Watering plants and weeding
  • Minor home repairs
  • Window cleaning & outdoor tidying
  • Light bulb changes & safety checks
  • Home safety assessments
Getting Started

How Home Care
Works

Starting home care is simple. We handle the complexity so you can focus on what matters — living well at home.

Start Today →
1

Get in Touch

Call us or submit an enquiry online. Our friendly team will have an initial conversation to understand your situation, needs, and what level of support would be right for you.

2

Your Personalised Care Plan

We'll create a care plan tailored specifically to you — covering which services you need, how often, and at what times. Everything is designed around your routine and preferences.

3

Meet Your Care Worker

We carefully match you with a care worker who suits your personality and needs. We aim for consistency — the same face, regularly, so you can build real trust over time.

4

Ongoing Support & Reviews

Your care plan is reviewed regularly as your needs change. We stay in close contact with you and your family to make sure everything is working and you're happy with your support.
