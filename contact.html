Frequently Asked

Common Questions

How do I start with Live Well Support? +

Simply contact us by phone, email, or via the form on this page. We'll arrange an initial conversation to understand your needs and how we can help. If you have an NDIS plan, have it handy when you call — but it's not required for your first contact.

Do I need an NDIS plan to access services? +

For NDIS services, yes — you need an approved NDIS plan. For aged care home care services, different funding pathways apply. Contact us and we'll help you understand your options and eligibility.

What areas do you service? +

We provide services across the greater Darwin region, including Darwin CBD, Palmerston, Tiwi, Casuarina, Rosebery, and surrounding suburbs. Contact us if you're unsure whether we cover your area.

Can I choose my support worker? +

Yes. We do our best to match you with a support worker who suits your personality, needs, and preferences. We also aim to provide consistent support workers so you build a genuine relationship over time.

How quickly can support be arranged? +

We work as quickly as possible to get support in place. For urgent situations, we do our best to fast-track arrangements. In most cases, support can begin within a few days of completing the intake process.
