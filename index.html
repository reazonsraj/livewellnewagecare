Understanding NDIS

Three Ways NDIS
Funds Your Life

The NDIS provides flexible funding across three support categories — designed to cover everything you need to live independently, participate in your community, and build lasting skills.

01
Core Supports
Everyday Support
The most flexible support category — covering personal care, household tasks, community access, and transport. Core funding can be used across categories to suit your day-to-day needs and goals.
02
Capital Supports
Equipment & Home Mods
One-off investments that create lasting independence — mobility aids, assistive technology, home modifications, and vehicle adaptations tailored to your specific disability and circumstances.
03
Capacity Building
Skills & Independence
Funding for therapy, training, and support coordination — helping you develop skills, build confidence, find employment, and participate more fully in community life and relationships.

Do I Qualify for the NDIS?

You need to meet all of these criteria to access NDIS funding

🇦🇺
Residency

Australian citizen, permanent resident, or Protected Special Category Visa holder

🎂
Age

Under 65 years old at the time of first applying for NDIS funding

♿
Disability

Permanent disability that significantly affects everyday activities and functioning

👶
Early Intervention

Under 9 with developmental delay or a condition likely to cause disability

📍
Location

Live in an area where NDIS operates — we serve Darwin, Palmerston & surrounds
