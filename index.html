Understanding NDIS

How NDIS
Works For You

The NDIS provides funding to help you live your best life. Started in 2013, it ensures everyone with a disability gets the support they need.

01
Core Supports
Everyday Support

The basic supports for daily activities — personal care, household tasks, and community access. Core supports are flexible, used where you need them most.

02
Capital Supports
Equipment & Modifications

One-off purchases that help achieve your goals — mobility aids, home modifications, and assistive technology to build independence.

03
Capacity Building
Skills & Independence

Training, therapy, and coordination to build your skills — empowering you to participate more fully in your community and employment.

Who Qualifies for the NDIS?

🇦🇺
Residency

Australian citizen, permanent resident, or Protected Special Category Visa holder

🎂
Age

Under 65 years old at the time of applying for the NDIS

♿
Disability

Permanent disability that significantly impacts everyday activities

👶
Early Intervention

Under 9 with developmental delay or condition likely to cause disability

📍
Location

Must live in an area where NDIS is available — we serve Darwin & surrounds
