Who We Are

People-First Care
From Darwin

Live Well Care And Support is a nationally registered NDIS provider and aged care service based in Darwin, Northern Territory. We are dedicated to providing high-quality, person-centred support to people with disabilities, older Australians, and their families.

We were built on a simple idea: that every person — regardless of age, ability, or background — deserves support that respects their dignity, upholds their choices, and helps them live the life they want. That idea still drives everything we do.

We serve participants and clients across Darwin, Palmerston, Tiwi, and Casuarina, delivering services that range from daily personal care to complex support coordination, community participation, and home maintenance.

Our Approach

Support That Starts
With Listening

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all care. Before we provide a single hour of support, we take the time to truly understand who you are — your goals, your challenges, your relationships, and the life you want to lead.

This understanding shapes every aspect of how we support you. Your care plan is a living document, reviewed regularly and updated as your needs and aspirations evolve.

  • In-depth intake and goal-setting conversations
  • Matching with support workers who suit your needs
  • Regular plan reviews and check-ins
  • Open communication with families and carers
  • Coordination with your NDIS plan manager and coordinators
