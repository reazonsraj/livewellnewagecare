How It Works

Simple Steps
to Get Started

Referring someone to Live Well is quick and straightforward. Complete the form and we'll take it from there.

1
Submit the referral form with as much information as you have. You don't need to have everything — we'll follow up to gather any missing details.
2
We review and respond within 1–2 business days to confirm receipt and discuss the referral with you.
3
We contact the participant (or their guardian) to arrange an intake conversation and understand their goals and needs.
4
A personalised support plan is created and support commences — usually within days of completing the intake.

Who Can Refer?

We welcome referrals from:

  • NDIS participants (self-referral)
  • Family members and carers
  • Support coordinators and plan managers
  • GPs and allied health professionals
  • Hospitals and discharge teams
  • Other disability or aged care services
